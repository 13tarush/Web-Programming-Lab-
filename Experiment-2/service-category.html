Types of Railway Services

Local Train

  • 🚉 Frequent stops
  • ⏱ Slower travel time compared to express
  • 👥 High passenger capacity
  • 💰 Most economical option

AC Local

  • ❄️ Air-conditioned coaches
  • 🪑 Comfortable seating
  • 🚉 Limited stops compared to regular local
  • 💳 Premium ticket pricing

Express / Premium

  • ⚡ Faster travel time
  • 🚉 Limited stops
  • 🕒 Time-efficient service
  • 💰 Higher fare
